🎯 What It Does

Detects Golden Cross events — when a fast moving average crosses above a slow one, signaling a potential long-term trend change.

⚠️ Important: This scanner uses 20-day MA crossing above 200-day MA — not the textbook 50/200. The 20-day crossover is more responsive but fires more often.

✅ Entry Criteria

  • 20 DMA crosses above 200 DMA within last 5 days (lookback = 5)
  • 200 DMA slope < max_200ma_slope = 5.0% (relatively flat — trend change, not acceleration)
  • Price currently above BOTH 20 DMA and 200 DMA
  • Price was ABOVE 200 SMA at the time of cross (40% success rate vs 18% below)
  • Volume on cross day ≥ 1.5x average (confirmation)
  • RSI ≥ 50 at cross (momentum present)
  • Avg volume ≥ min_volume = 100,000
  • Signal strength ≥ min_signal_strength = 60

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below 200-day SMA
  • Target: 20-50%+ over 6-12 months
  • Holding Period: 6-12 months (long-term trend)
  • Position Size: 2-5% (larger due to high historical quality)

📝 Summary

Six-component score with minimum floor of 60. Uses 20/200 (not classic 50/200). Requires a flat 200 DMA — catches genuine regime changes, filters out accelerations of existing trends.

🎯 What It Does

Implements the StockBee Episodic Pivot methodology — detects gap-ups driven by a fresh catalyst on stocks that were "neglected" (tight price base) prior to the gap. Two-tier output: CONFIRMED vs WATCHLIST.

💡 Key Concept: A true EP = gap + volume + catalyst + tight base + market regime. Missing any ingredient drops the trade to watchlist or skips it.

🌐 Market Regime Filter (Qullamaggie)

  • Bullish: QQQ 10 EMA > 20 EMA AND both rising → green light

  • Neutral/Unknown: normal sizing

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below gap-up low or prior day close (typically 3-7%)
  • Position Size: Scale with EP score — max only if CONFIRMED + bullish regime + score > 80
  • Holding Period: 1-4 weeks (momentum fades quickly)

📝 Summary

Eight-component weighted score fused with a two-tier classifier. Most complex scanner in the suite. Production use requires integration with news/catalyst feed. CONFIRMED candidates are trade-ready; WATCHLIST requires further confirmation.

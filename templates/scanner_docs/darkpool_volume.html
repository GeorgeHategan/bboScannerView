🎯 What It Does

Detects institutional dark pool volume spikes — large off-exchange trades that reveal hidden accumulation or distribution before the broader market sees it.

💡 Key Concept: Dark pool trades happen off-exchange so institutions can accumulate large positions without moving the price. A sudden spike in dark pool volume often precedes major moves.

📡 Data Source

Reads pre-aggregated signals from MotherDuck table options_data.darkpool_signals (date, ticker, direction, dp_volume, dp_premium, buy_sell_ratio, avg_block_size, etc.).

✅ Entry Criteria

  • Volume ratio ≥ MIN_VOLUME_RATIO = 5.0x (vs 20-day average)
  • Dark pool premium ≥ MIN_PREMIUM = $1,000,000
  • Bullish percentage ≥ MIN_BULLISH_PCT = 60% OR direction = BULLISH
  • Block size analysis (avg_block_size) for institutional confirmation

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: 3-5% below signal day low
  • Position Size: Smaller — dark pool signals are leading but can precede moves by days to weeks
  • Holding Period: 1-4 weeks (needs time for institutional positioning to manifest)
  • Combine with: Technical setup from another scanner for entry timing

📝 Summary

Reads pre-aggregated dark pool signals; filters for 5x+ volume spike, $1M+ premium, 60%+ bullish bias. Four-component signal strength scoring. Best used as a conviction overlay — not primary entry signal.

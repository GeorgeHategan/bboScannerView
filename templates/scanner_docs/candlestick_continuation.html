🎯 What It Does

Detects TA-Lib continuation candlestick patterns — signals that an existing uptrend will persist. Unlike the reversal scanner, this REQUIRES an established uptrend.

📈 Uptrend Classification

strong_uptrend: price > SMA20 AND price > SMA50 AND SMA20_slope > 5% AND SMA50_slope > 2%
moderate_uptrend: price > SMA20 AND price > SMA50
weak_uptrend: price > SMA20 OR price > SMA50
no_uptrend: skipped (continuation requires existing trend)

✅ Entry Criteria

  • ≥1 continuation pattern detected
  • Trend strength ≥ weak_uptrend (required)
  • Symbol in picked_symbols (last 30 days, other scanners)
  • Excludes other candlestick scanners

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: 2-4% below entry (tighter than reversal — trend already established)
  • Position Size: 90+=3%, 80-89=2%, 65-79=1%
  • Hold Period: 1-3 weeks

🎯 What It Does

Detects explosive short-term momentum moves — runs three parallel detectors at 1, 3, and 5-day timeframes, each with its own gain and volume thresholds. Inspired by the Stockbee methodology.

💡 Key Concept: A momentum burst is a regime shift — the stock goes from quiet to explosive in a single session. The gain is only half the signal: it has to happen on abnormal volume, because a 5% move on average volume is noise, while a 5% move on 3× volume is institutional positioning.

📈 Core Concepts

1. Three Parallel Detectors (1D / 3D / 5D)

The scanner runs three independent detectors, each with scaled gain+volume thresholds. A stock can qualify on any one (or multiple). Shorter timeframes demand higher relative volume; longer timeframes demand larger cumulative gains.

2. Trend Pre-Filter (Price > SMA 20)

Only stocks trading above their 20-day SMA qualify. This prevents the scanner from firing on oversold-bounces in broken charts — a 4% pop on a downtrending stock is a dead-cat bounce, not momentum.

Impact: The SMA distance is also scored: ≥15% above SMA20 = 20 pts; ≥10% = 15; ≥5% = 10; else = 5.

3. Liquidity Floor (Dollar Volume)

Current daily dollar volume must be ≥ $50M. Lower-liquidity stocks can produce technically valid bursts but are unexecutable in size — the scanner excludes them to keep signals tradable.

4. Timeframe Bonus (Rewards Short Frames)

1-day bursts get +10 score bonus; 3-day bursts +7; 5-day bursts +5. Rationale: shorter-frame bursts are rarer and more actionable (entry window is ~1 day before the move continues or fades).

5. Magnitude Scoring (Rewards Larger Moves)

Move magnitude is scored in tiers: 12%+ = 40 pts (max), 10% = 35, 8% = 30, 6% = 25, smaller = 20. This is the largest single component at 40% of the total.

✅ Entry Criteria (all timeframes)

  • Meets at least one of the three (gain, volume) pairs above
  • Price above SMA 20 (confirmed uptrend)
  • Stock price $5 ≤ price ≤ $500
  • Current daily dollar volume ≥ $50,000,000
  • Market cap ≥ $50M

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: 5-7% below entry — momentum can reverse fast
  • Position Size: 0.5-1% risk per trade (high volatility)
  • Holding Period: 1-5 days for 1D bursts, 1-3 weeks for 5D bursts
  • Exit Rule: Scale out on first down day following the burst

📝 Summary

Three parallel detectors with tiered gain+volume thresholds, offset by an SMA trend gate and a $50M liquidity floor. Shorter timeframes get a score bonus because they're rarer and more actionable. No RSI filter (contrary to legacy documentation).

🎯 What It Does

Detects explosive short-term momentum moves across 3 timeframes (1/3/5 days), each with its own gain + volume requirements. Stockbee-inspired methodology.

⚡ Three Signal Types

TimeframeMin GainVol MultiplierCharacter
1-day burst≥ 4.0%≥ 2.0x avgMost explosive
3-day burst≥ 6.0%≥ 1.5x 3-day avgSustained momentum
5-day burst≥ 8.0%≥ 1.3x 5-day avgStrong sustained

✅ Entry Criteria (all timeframes)

  • Meets one of the three (gain, volume) pairs above
  • Price above SMA 20 (confirmed uptrend)
  • Stock price $5 ≤ price ≤ $500
  • Current daily dollar volume ≥ $50,000,000
  • Market cap ≥ $50M (liquidity)

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: 5-7% below entry (momentum can reverse fast)
  • Position Size: 0.5-1% risk per trade (high volatility)
  • Holding Period: 1-5 days for 1D bursts, 1-3 weeks for 5D bursts
  • Exit Rule: Scale out on first down day following the burst

📝 Summary

Three parallel detectors (1/3/5-day) with different gain+volume thresholds. Shorter timeframes get a score bonus since they're rarer and more actionable. No RSI filter despite what older docs said.

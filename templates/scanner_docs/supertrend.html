🎯 What It Does

Detects fresh bullish SuperTrend flips — moments when the volatility-adjusted SuperTrend band flips from bearish (above price) to bullish (below price), marking the potential start of a new trend leg. Scored across four components for quality filtering.

💡 Key Concept: SuperTrend is an ATR-based trailing stop that adapts to volatility. When price is above the line, it acts as dynamic support; when below, as resistance. The flip (where the line jumps from above to below price) is the signal — it means volatility-adjusted buyers have taken control.

📈 Core Concepts

1. SuperTrend Calculation (ATR-Based Bands)

SuperTrend uses two bands: upper = hl2 + multiplier × ATR, lower = hl2 - multiplier × ATR, where hl2 = (high + low) / 2. The scanner uses SUPERTREND_PERIOD = 10 for ATR and SUPERTREND_MULTIPLIER = 3.0.

Direction rule: When price crosses above the upper band, trend flips bullish. When it crosses below the lower band, trend flips bearish. The active band then acts as a trailing stop.

Formula: ST_upper = (H + L) / 2 + 3.0 × ATR(10)

2. Freshness Gate (Flip Within Last 3 Days)

The bullish flip must have occurred within MAX_DAYS_SINCE_FLIP = 3 days. Older flips are discarded — by day 4+ the setup has usually resolved and entering gives back most of the intended move.

Impact: Day-of-flip scores 25 pts; 1 day old = 20; 2 days = 15; 3+ days = 10.

3. Flip-Day Volume Confirmation

Volume on the flip day should be ≥ 1.2× the recent average. Low-volume flips are more likely to whipsaw back.

Impact: ≥ 2.0× = 25 pts; ≥ 1.5× = 20; ≥ 1.2× = 15; ≥ 1.0× = 10; else = 5.

4. Distance-to-Line (Sweet Spot 1-5%)

The distance between current price and the SuperTrend line is scored with a sweet spot. Too close (< 0.5%) is a weak signal that might immediately re-cross; too far (> 8%) means the move has already happened.

Tiers: 1-5% = 25 pts (ideal); 0.5-1% = 20; 5-8% = 15; < 0.5% = 10; > 8% = 5.

5. Gain-Since-Flip (Reward Patient Entries)

Modest gains (0-3%) since the flip score highest — meaning you're early, not chasing. Zero/negative gains still score 25 (very early); 3-5% = 20; larger gains = only 10 (already extended).

✅ Entry Criteria

  • SuperTrend just flipped bullish within last 3 days (MAX_DAYS_SINCE_FLIP = 3)
  • Price currently above SuperTrend line (supertrend_direction == 1)
  • Stock price ≥ MIN_PRICE = $5.00
  • Avg dollar volume ≥ MIN_AVG_DOLLAR_VOLUME = $50,000,000

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below SuperTrend line itself (natural volatility-adjusted stop)
  • Trailing Stop: Exit on bearish SuperTrend flip
  • Position Size: Normal (1-2% risk)
  • Holding Period: Weeks to months (trend-following)

📝 Summary

Four-component equal-weight scoring (25 pts each) on fresh bullish SuperTrend flips. ATR-based stop means the system self-adjusts to volatility. Best held until an opposing flip — which is also the natural exit signal.

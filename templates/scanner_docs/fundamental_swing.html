⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below SMA 50 or recent swing low (~5-8%)
  • Target: Analyst target price or 15-30% upside
  • Holding Period: 14-30 days (swing)
  • Position Size: Larger than momentum scans — quality-based, lower turnover

⚠️ Known Issue

Calibration note: Historical results show most signals scoring exactly 50.0 — indicates some components may be producing default values when fundamental data is missing. Review data completeness before relying on the composite score.

📝 Summary

Weighted composite of 7 fundamental and 5 technical factors. Threshold 60 required; 80+ excellent. Best for longer-horizon swing trades where both quality and timing matter. Do not use for short-term momentum plays.

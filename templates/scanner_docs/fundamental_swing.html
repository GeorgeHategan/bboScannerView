🎯 What It Does

A hybrid quality + timing scanner. Combines seven fundamental factors (60% weight) with five technical entry factors (40% weight) into a single composite score. Targets swing trades with 14-30 day holding horizons where both the company quality and the entry timing matter.

💡 Key Concept: Pure-technical scanners catch fast moves but include garbage companies. Pure-fundamental screens find great companies but terrible entry points. This scanner demands both. The 60/40 weight reflects that what you buy matters more than when — but timing still determines whether your stop holds.

📈 Core Concepts

1. Valuation Cluster (P/E + PEG)

Ideal P/E: 10-20 (20 pts). Ideal PEG: below 1.0 (15 pts). P/E alone is noisy; PEG normalizes it against growth. Stocks scoring well on both are reasonably priced and growing — the classic Peter Lynch GARP setup.

2. Profitability Cluster (Margin + ROE)

Profit margin ≥ 20% = 15 pts. ROE ≥ 20% = 15 pts. These two together filter for companies that turn revenue into profit and reinvest that profit at high returns — the hallmark of compounding businesses.

3. Balance Sheet + Income (Debt + Dividend + Analyst Upside)

Debt/Equity < 30 = 15 pts. Dividend yield 2-5% = 10 pts. Analyst target upside ≥ 20% = 10 pts. These three round out the fundamental score with solvency, shareholder returns, and forward-looking sentiment.

4. Trend Alignment (30 pts — largest technical component)

Requires price > SMA 20 > SMA 50 > SMA 200. All four in stack order. This is the strongest possible trend confirmation on a single timeframe.

Logic: Quality companies in downtrends keep going down. The stack filter prevents "value trap" entries.

5. RSI Sweet Spot + MACD

RSI 14 in 40-60 range = 25 pts (not overbought, not oversold). MACD above signal line = 20 pts (momentum aligned). Together they catch pullback-to-MA entries rather than chase-the-breakout entries.

6. Volume + Pullback Confirmation

Recent volume ≥ 1.2× 20-day average = 15 pts (accumulation showing up). Distance from SMA 20 in -5% to +2% range = 10 pts (fresh pullback entry, not extended).

⚠️ Known Issue

Calibration note: Historical results show most signals scoring exactly 50.0 — indicates some fundamental components may default to a neutral value when upstream data is missing. Review fundamental data completeness before relying on the composite score for production trades.

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below SMA 50 or recent swing low (~5-8%)
  • Target: Analyst target price or 15-30% upside
  • Holding Period: 14-30 days (swing)
  • Position Size: Larger than momentum scans — quality-based, lower turnover

📝 Summary

Weighted 7-fundamental + 5-technical composite. Best for longer-horizon swing trades where company quality matters alongside timing. Not suitable for short-term momentum plays — for those, use momentum_burst or breakout scanners.

🎯 What It Does

Detects the classic Cup and Handle pattern — a rounded "cup" base followed by a shallow "handle" pullback before breakout. One of the most reliable William O'Neil / IBD chart patterns.

🪜 4-Phase Detection

Phase 1 — Cup Formation: Left rim high → rounded low → right rim. Depth 10-30%, duration 20-60 days.
Phase 2 — Cup Quality: U-shape verified (variance < 3% per side), right rim ≥ 80% of left rim height.
Phase 3 — Handle Formation: Tight pullback off right rim. Duration 5-20 days, depth 3-15% of cup right rim.
Phase 4 — Breakout Proximity: Price near handle high, RVOL ≥ 0.8, pattern ≤ 10 days old.

✅ Entry Criteria

  • Quality score ≥ quality_threshold = 50 (relaxed from 65)
  • Right rim ≥ 80% of left rim height (relaxed from 90%)
  • RVOL ≥ min_rvol = 0.8 (relaxed from 1.0)
  • Cup depth 10-30%, duration 20-60 days
  • Handle depth 3-15%, duration 5-20 days

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below handle low (typically 3-5% below entry)
  • Target: Cup depth projected up from breakout (measured move)
  • Position Size: 90+=3%, 80-89=2%, 70-79=1%
  • Holding Period: 4-12 weeks

📝 Summary

Four-phase structural pattern detection with 5-component quality scoring. Thresholds intentionally relaxed (quality 50+, rvol 0.8+, right rim 80%+) for higher signal count. U-shape verification distinguishes cups from V-bottoms.

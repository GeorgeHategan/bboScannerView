🎯 What It Does

Detects TA-Lib bullish reversal candlestick patterns — single-, two-, and three-bar formations that historically mark the exhaustion of a downtrend and the start of a new uptrend. This scanner runs as a confirmation layer: it only analyzes symbols already picked by other scanners in the last 30 days.

💡 Key Concept: A reversal candlestick alone is noise. A reversal candlestick at a setup another scanner already flagged is signal. This scanner is architected around that principle — it never fires standalone.

📈 Core Concepts

1. Curated Pattern Library (11 of 61 TA-Lib patterns)

TA-Lib ships 61 candlestick recognizers. This scanner keeps only the 11 with the strongest historical reversal reliability, each assigned a weight from 5.0 (marginal) to 10.0 (premium). Weaker or ambiguous patterns (Doji, Hanging Man, etc.) are deliberately excluded to reduce noise.

Logic: Pattern weight is the base of the signal strength score — stronger patterns inherit a higher score floor.

2. Recency Filter

Only the most recent recent_bars_to_check = 3 bars are inspected for patterns. Older patterns have lost their information value — by the time a Morning Star is 5 days old, the setup has either resolved or gone stale.

3. Downtrend Context (Trend Score Bonus)

A reversal is only meaningful if there's something to reverse. The scanner checks whether the recent 5-day low sits below the prior 15-day low — if yes, the stock was genuinely making new lows, and a +5 "trend context" bonus is added.

4. Volume Confirmation

Patterns with volume ≥ 1.5× the recent 5-day average score +10; patterns with at least average volume score +5. Volume-less reversal patterns are real but less reliable.

Formula: today_volume / mean(last_5_days_volume) ≥ 1.5

5. Pattern Stacking

If multiple patterns fire on the same bar (e.g. Engulfing + Hammer), the scanner rewards the cluster: +5 for two, +10 for three, +15 for four or more. Stacked patterns compound the reversal evidence.

✅ Entry Criteria

  • ≥1 bullish reversal pattern detected within last 3 bars (recent_bars_to_check = 3)
  • Primary pattern weight ≥ MIN_SCORE = 7.5
  • Symbol must appear in picked_symbols (other scanners' output, last 30 days)
  • Excludes: candlestick_bullish, candlestick_continuation, candlestick (avoids circular confirmation)

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Stop Loss: Below the pattern low (3-5% typical)
  • Position Size: 90+=3%, 80-89=2%, 70-79=1%
  • Hold Period: 1-4 weeks (reversals need time to develop a new trend)
  • Exit Rule: Exit if a new trend fails to form within 5-7 bars

📝 Summary

Curated 11-pattern library × 4-component scoring, acting only as a confirmation layer on top of other scanners. Because it's gated by picked_symbols, it never fires standalone — this is the scanner's signature design choice and the reason its signals tend to be high-conviction.
